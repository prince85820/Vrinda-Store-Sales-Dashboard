# 📊 Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report 2026

![Dashboard Preview](Dashboard.png)

## 📌 Overview

The **Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report 2026** is an interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze annual sales performance, customer demographics, regional sales, and order fulfillment. It enables businesses to monitor key performance indicators (KPIs) and make data-driven decisions through dynamic visualizations and slicers.

---

## 🎯 Project Objective

The objective of this project is to:

- Analyze annual sales performance.
- Compare total orders with total sales.
- Identify top-performing states.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior by age and gender.
- Monitor order delivery performance.
- Analyze sales across multiple sales channels.
- Build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📂 Dashboard Overview

### 📈 Orders vs Sales
- Compares total sales amount with total orders.
- Helps track overall business performance.

### 👩‍🦰 Sales by Gender
- Women: **62%**
- Men: **38%**

### 📦 Order Status
- Delivered: **94%**
- Returned: **3%**
- Cancelled: **2%**
- Refunded: **1%**

### 🏆 Top 5 Sales States
- Maharashtra
- Karnataka
- Uttar Pradesh
- Tamil Nadu
- Telangana

### 👥 Orders by Age & Gender
Customer analysis across:
- Adult
- Teenager
- Senior

### 🎛 Interactive Filters
Users can filter dashboard data using:
- Product Category
- Sales Channel
- Month

---

## 📊 Key Insights

- Women contribute the majority of total sales (**62%**).
- **94%** of orders are successfully delivered.
- Maharashtra is the highest revenue-generating state.
- Adult customers generate the highest number of orders.
- Interactive slicers allow users to analyze sales by month, category, and sales channel.
